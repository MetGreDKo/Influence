Nation simulators have been a passion of mine since 2000. I've typically played them pen and paper online in small online communities. Unfortunately as we all have gone through life we lack the time to give the games the love and affection they so deserve. The creation of this site and a new game within it is my effort to keep it alive and bring back something I had spent many hours on.

Other games of the sort have had a number of names from Super Power, Global Conflict, Defcon and Head of State being just a few I've seen in these communities. They all had a heavy emphasis on military power with significant diplomatic efforts. The concepts they all sought to explore were rudimentary due to the nature of the games being pen and paper. Here however I hope to change that.

This game is called Influence. My wish is to build the basis for a deep and complex experience in an approachable manner so that there is always something to be done to improve a nation and some way to target or otherwise attack it beyond the military. I want everything to have some impact whether on its own or coupled with other things. I essentially want to feel like it's a living world where choices matter.

Influence is a game which takes place on Earth with technology from 1900. Players can create their own nations in what ever image they desire. There is no goal inherent to the game besides that of running said nation. You may seek to conquer the world or try to work with those within to try and maintain an equilibrium. Alternatively you may just build the very best nation you can or create a true dystopia. This project is in its infancy so actually coded options are still limited. There are plans for a great many things but due to my novice level in coding I ask that patience be had.

Registering an account with subsequent accessing and usage of it does mean certain information is recorded and cookies are utilized. As you can see there are no advertisements to this site at present and I intend for that to remain true throughout its life. This is a passion project and not something I ever wish to be sullied. The data I collect will never be shared with anyone by myself unless legally compelled by a court or other such process in the United States of America. That said there may be occasions others are brought in to assist and they may have access.
